## New Workflows

I think there is a great possibility to create a workflow that goes:

1. Find jobs on Upwork (would need to be a local browser)
2. Find the CEO of the company posting
3. Build an example implementation of their project for free
4. Cold email them offering to implement it for free and if you do a good job then they leave a review.

Would be a great way to get clients to start out and escape upwork stupidness. Thankfully Upwork has done such a great job of cementing their brand as the first place that people should look for odd jobs that a marketplace has formed of poeple looking for this type of work.
